Rules

▼
  • Order wins by creating a line of 5 identical symbols (X or O) in any direction
  • Chaos wins by filling the board without Order achieving 5 in a row
  • Both players can place either X or O on their turn
  • The game is played on a 6×6 grid

Game Reference: Order and Chaos - Wikipedia

Smart AI Strategy

▼

Order AI Strategy: Uses the proven "center 4×4 control" strategy, focusing on positions in rows 1-4 and columns 1-4. Creates multiple simultaneous threats (forks) and employs edge-pairing counters to neutralize Chaos edge moves. Research shows this gives Order a forced win with perfect play.

Chaos AI Strategy: Implements advanced pairing strategy to systematically block all potential 5-in-a-rows. Prioritizes critical threat blocking, prevents open-four formations, and disrupts Order's center control using sophisticated position evaluation.
